Online Game Addiction

Online game addiction is when a person cannot control the time they spend playing online games. It often makes them play for long hours even when they should be doing other activities. This behavior can affect their studies and responsibilities. Many addicted players ignore sleep and rest just to keep playing. It can also reduce time spent with family and friends. Some people feel stressed or angry when they are not allowed to play. Over time, it may lead to poor health because of lack of exercise and sleep. It can also affect focus and performance in school or work. Online games are designed to be engaging, which can make them hard to stop playing. Managing screen time and setting limits can help prevent this problem.

Symptoms

  • Too much gaming
  • Ignoring schoolwork
  • Anger when not playing

Effects

  • Low grades
  • Sleep loss
  • Less social life

Prevention

  • Limit gaming time
  • Focus on studies
  • Do hobbies
